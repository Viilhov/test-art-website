Education

1960-1965 Camden Grammar School for Girls, London.
1965-1960 Courses in Renaissance Art and Architecture at the British Institute, Florence, Italy. Studied Life Drawing at the School of Fine Arts, Florence.
1988 Attended Lynn le Grice's stencilling course, Falmouth, Cornwall.
1998-1999 Jewellery and silversmithing at Lancaster and Morecambe College.
1999.2001 Jewellery at the Brewery Arts Centre, Kendal, Cumbria.

Qualifications

Mostly self taught.
'A'levels in Art and Italian

Solo Exhibitions

1986 The Brewery Arts Centre, Kendal. Cumbria.
1987 Theatre in the Forest Grizedale.

Group Exhibitions

2000 Green Door Studios Annual Exhibition.
Local Art Society exhibitions including Sedbergh and Milnthorpe.

Private Comissions

1993 Gulbenkein Foundation commission to work on a mural for the People's Hall, Sedbergh, with the local community. Involved 6 week's teaching culminating in a one-day workshop.
1997 Mural for a conservatory based on an Italianate theme.
1998-2001 Many interior paint effects for kitchens and living rooms.

Other

1968-1971 Assistant Manager, Heal's Art Gallery, Heal's, London.
1973-1975 Abbot Hall ArtGallery, Kendal.
Organiser of touring exhibitions service and standing in for the Director for slide shows.
Hanging exhibitions.
